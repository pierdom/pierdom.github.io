I am an experienced Data Scientist with Computer Engineering background. Currently, I work as Technical Lead in the Data team of Typeform, focusing on product and customer analytics in the SaaS field. Before, I have worked at Zeelo, a start-up in the transportation market, where I led the data activities end-to-end, including development of smart data products, advanced analytics and BI.

My PhD studies at the Technical University of Vienna (TUWien) and the years spent in R&D centres helped me complementing my hands-on profile with a theoretical and research attitude. During my PhD, I have authored more than 30 scientific publications in major international conferences and journals.
